PUT THE PORTALS

Introduction

Put the Portals is an indie game, set in a magical forest, where the player must solve puzzles using portals to defeat his enemies. The game was developed within the Apple Developer Academy program in partnership with the Universidade Católica de Brasília in Brazil.

Description

Put the portals for you to move around the map, destroying all enemies and achieving your victory. Remember the primary rule of portal creators, never put a portal without its par.

Special items

You will certainly face complex challenges, in these moments use the special items, which can show the initial path of the challenge, trace your routes through the portals or even stop the time so that you can solve the challenge without worrying about duration.
